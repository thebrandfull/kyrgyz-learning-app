## Important Notes

1. **Formal vs Informal**: Kyrgyz has formal (сыз/суз endings) and informal (сың/сиң endings) forms
   - Use formal with strangers, elders, or in professional settings
   - Use informal with friends and family

2. **Vowel Harmony**: Kyrgyz follows vowel harmony rules - suffixes change based on the vowels in the root word

3. **Common Mistakes to Avoid**:
   - Don't translate "how are you" literally - use "Кандайсыз?" not a word-for-word translation
   - "Hello" is "Салам" not a literal translation from Russian or English
   - Many greetings are time-specific (morning, afternoon, evening)
